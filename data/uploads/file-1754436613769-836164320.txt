COMPANY POLICY DOCUMENT

Remote Work Policy:
All employees are eligible for remote work arrangements with manager approval. Remote work days must be scheduled in advance and communicated to the team.

Time Off Policy:
- New employees: 15 vacation days annually
- Employees with 2+ years: 20 vacation days annually
- Sick leave: 10 days annually
- Personal days: 3 days annually

Benefits Overview:
- Health insurance: Medical, dental, and vision coverage
- 401(k) retirement plan with company matching
- Professional development budget: $2,000 annually
- Flexible spending account available

Performance Review Process:
Annual performance reviews are conducted in December. Mid-year check-ins occur in June. Career development discussions are encouraged quarterly.

Code of Conduct:
All employees must maintain professional behavior and adhere to ethical business practices. Harassment and discrimination are strictly prohibited.
